DEREGISTRATION OF PLAN

In its Registration Statement on Form S-8 (File No. 333-124256) (the Registration Statement), Dominion Energy, Inc. (formerly Dominion Resources, Inc.) (Dominion Energy) registered 30,000,000 shares of its common stock (15,000,000 shares prior to the two-for-one stock split approved by the Board of Directors on November 19, 2007), without par value, for issuance to participants in the Dominion Resources, Inc. 2005 Incentive Compensation Plan (the Plan). In its Post-Effective Amendment No. 1 to the Registration Statement, Dominion Energy deregistered 25,304,147 shares of common stock as to which no incentive awards were outstanding and which, consequently, would not be issued under the Plan.   As of October 1, 2019, no incentive awards remain outstanding and Dominion Energy is filing this Post-Effective Amendment No. 2 to the Registration Statement to terminate all offerings under the Registration Statement.
