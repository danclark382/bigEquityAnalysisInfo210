Item 8.01    Other Events.

On April 18, 2018, Visa Inc. (the "Company"), issued a press release announcing that on April 17, 2018, its board of directors declared a quarterly cash dividend in the aggregate amount of $0.21 per share of class A common stock (determined in the case of class B and C common stock and series B and C convertible participating preferred stock on an as-converted basis), payable on June 5, 2018, to all holders of record as of May 18, 2018.

A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K.
