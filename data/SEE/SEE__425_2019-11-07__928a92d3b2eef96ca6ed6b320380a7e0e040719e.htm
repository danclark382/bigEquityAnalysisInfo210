The following communication was emailed to employees of Sprint:

 

Dear partners,

T-Mobile has become known for its Un-carrier moves, and today we have reason to join in the excitement because they made their first Un-carrier announcement for the New T-Mobile!

As you know, merging Sprint and T-Mobile has many benefits that are built around combining our networks into a supercharged, truly nationwide 5G network – and using that coverage and capacity to work for good.

Today’s New T-Mobile Un-carrier 1.0 announcement unveils three bold initiatives:

 

  •  

Connecting Heroes Initiative, a 10-year commitment to provide free unlimited 5G service to all public and nonprofit U.S. state and local law enforcement, fire and EMS agencies.

 

  •  

Project 10Million, a new program – expanding on the vision Marcelo started with the 1Million Project – designed to eradicate the homework gap that exists for millions of children. Project 10Million proposes to offer free service, hotspots and reduced-cost devices to 10 million households over five years – a $10 billion commitment. With the unprecedented scale of this effort, the New T-Mobile has the chance to build on the great work of the 1Million Project Foundation and T-Mobile’s EmpowerED to impact millions more people across the country.

 

  •  

T-Mobile Connect, a groundbreaking prepaid service offering that will bring a new, competitive option to lower-income consumers. It will start at just $15 a month for unlimited talk, text and 2GB of high-speed data.

 

New T-Mobile is going to change wireless for GOOD, and I hope you are as
energized as I am to stand behind these amazing efforts.

 

But who better to pump us up about the first Un-carrier move of the New
T-Mobile than John Legere, CEO of T-Mobile, and Mike Sievert, president
and COO of T-Mobile?!

   

Check out this fun video message, on YouTube or PixelFlix, that they shared with me. I can’t wait for you to see it.

There are so many opportunities for all of you on the Sprint Team at the New T-Mobile, and this is just a sneak peek of what’s to come when we combine two disruptive companies. I hope the possibilities excite you for the future!
