The following communication was emailed to employees of Sprint:

 

Dear partners,

Today T-Mobile announced some leadership changes that I am excited to share with all of you.

 

Its board of directors has named Mike Sievert as chief executive officer, beginning May 1, 2020. Mike currently serves as president, chief operating officer and a board director. His new title will be president and CEO.

Current T-Mobile CEO John Legere will continue his role through the end of his contract on April 30, 2020, and he will remain a member of the T-Mobile board.

In the meantime, Marcelo and I remain focused on working with John and Mike to successfully close the Sprint and T-Mobile merger.

I know you have been anxiously awaiting news of the leadership team for the new T-Mobile, but I’m asking for your patience a little longer.

 

 

As I have said in the past, the new company will include leaders from both companies, and we will share the details when the time is right.

Today’s announcement is part of T-Mobile’s years-long succession plan, and I’m thrilled that Mike was chosen to lead the company forward as it focuses on combining with Sprint.

I have really enjoyed my interactions with Mike in the past few months. He is a genuine and smart leader who has been super successful with John in transforming T-Mobile and executing its disruptive, Un-carrier strategy. And he cares deeply about connecting with his employees and customers.

I can’t wait to see the enthusiasm that Mike will bring to the New T-Mobile as he rallies employees from both companies to continue challenging the status quo and disrupting the wireless industry – and beyond.

Congratulations to John and Mike!

 

Michel Combes

CEO
