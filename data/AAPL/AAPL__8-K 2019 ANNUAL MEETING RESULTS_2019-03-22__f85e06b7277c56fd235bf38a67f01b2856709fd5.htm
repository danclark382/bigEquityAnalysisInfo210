Item 5.07Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders

 

The Annual Meeting of Stockholders of Agilent Technologies, Inc. (the “Company”) was held on March 20, 2019 (the “Annual Meeting”).  A total of 277,720,224 shares of Common Stock, representing approximately 87% of the shares outstanding, were represented at the Annual Meeting.  The voting results for each item of business properly presented at the Annual Meeting, as certified by the Company’s independent inspector of elections, are set forth below:  

 

 

Proposal No. 1

The election of three (3) directors for a term of three years.  The individuals listed below received the affirmative vote of a majority of the votes cast by the shares present in person or represented by proxy and entitled to vote at the Annual Meeting, and were each elected to serve a three-year term.
