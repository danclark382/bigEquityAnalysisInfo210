Eni’s Board of Directors

 

Approval of 2018 interim dividend: € 0.42 per share

 

Rome, 13 September 2018 – Eni’s Board of Directors today resolved to distribute to Shareholders an interim dividend for the fiscal year 2018 of € 0.42 per share outstanding at the ex-dividend date as of 24 September 20181, payable on 26 September 2018. The proposal to distribute an interim dividend of € 0.42 was announced to the market on 27 July 20182, when the Company published its second quarter results.

Holders of ADRs, outstanding at the record date of 25 September 2018, will receive € 0.84 per ADR, payable on 11 October 20183, with each ADR listed on the New York Stock Exchange representing two Eni shares.

Eni’s independent auditors have issued their report pursuant to Article 2433-bis, paragraph 5, of the Italian Civil Code.

The Board’s resolution on the interim dividend was made on the basis of the accounts of the parent company Eni SpA at 30 June 2018, which were prepared in accordance with IFRS. In the first half of 2018 Eni SpA reported net profit of € 2,976 million4.
