Item 1. Description of Registrant’s Securities to be Registered.

This Registration Statement relates to the 6.20% Notes due June 1, 2059 (the "Notes") issued by Ford Motor Company (the "Company"). The Notes are described in the Company's Prospectus Supplement dated May 20, 2019, to its Prospectus dated February 17, 2017, under the heading "Description of Notes". The Prospectus Supplement and the related Prospectus were filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the "SEC") pursuant to the Company's Registration Statement No. 333-216126 by the Company pursuant to Rule 424(b)(2) on May 21, 2019 and February 17, 2017, respectively.
