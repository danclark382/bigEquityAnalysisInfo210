Item 2.02. Results of Operations and Financial Condition.

Ford Motor Company (“Ford” or “Company”) hereby incorporates by reference its news release dated April 25, 2019, which is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 hereto.

Beginning at 5:30 p.m. on April 25, 2019, Ford President and Chief Executive Officer Jim Hackett, Chief Financial Officer Bob Shanks, and members of Ford’s senior management will host a conference call to discuss Ford’s first quarter 2019 financial results. Investors may access the presentation by dialing 1-877-870-8664 (or 1-970-297-2423 from outside the United States). The passcode for the call is a verbal response of “Ford Earnings.”

The presentation and supporting materials are available at www.shareholder.ford.com. Representatives of the investment community and the news media will have the opportunity to ask questions on the call.

Investors also may access replays of the presentation beginning after 9:30 p.m. the day of the event through May 2, 2019 by dialing 1-855-859-2056 (or 1-404-537-3406 from outside the United States). The Conference ID for replays is 6640339.

All times referenced above are in Eastern Time.
