Item 8.01

Other Events.

On October 2, 2018, Intelsat S.A. (“Intelsat”) issued a press release announcing that Intelsat Jackson Holdings S.A. (“Intelsat Jackson”), an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Intelsat, intends to offer an additional $700 million aggregate principal amount of 8.500% senior notes due 2024 (the “Notes”) in a new private offering. A copy of the press release is filed as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and is incorporated by reference herein. The Notes will be issued pursuant to an indenture dated September 19, 2018, among Intelsat Jackson, as issuer, the guarantors party thereto, and U.S. Bank National Association, as trustee (the “Indenture”). Intelsat Jackson had previously issued $2,250 million aggregate principal amount of its 8.500% senior notes due 2024 under the Indenture (the “Existing Notes”). The Notes constitute a further issuance of, and will be fungible with, the Existing Notes (except for any Notes sold pursuant to Regulation S for a period of 40 days after the issue date) and will be treated as a single series of securities for all purposes under the Indenture.

In connection with the offering of the Notes, Intelsat Jackson expects to issue a conditional notice of redemption to holders of its outstanding 7.500% senior notes due 2021.
